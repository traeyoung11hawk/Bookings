Our Business

WHAT IS
FOCUS
POD™?

FocusPod™ is a student-founded business dedicated to helping students improve their concentration and productivity while studying in libraries and other learning environments.

Our main product — the FocusPod — is a private study pod designed to minimise distractions and create an ideal space for studying. By providing students with a quiet, comfortable, and convenient study space, FocusPod students stay focused and achieve their goals.

🎧

Premium Noise-Cancelling Headphones

Block out surrounding noise completely. Each pod includes a high-quality pair of noise-cancelling headphones so you can study in pure silence.

⚡

Built-In Power Outlet

Never run out of battery mid-session. Every FocusPod comes equipped with a dedicated power outlet to charge your laptop, phone, or tablet.

🔒

Enclosed Privacy Walls

Curved walls block visual distractions from other people and activities nearby, creating your own private study bubble.

📚

Open to All Year Groups

Unlike the current study spaces limited to Year 11 and 12, FocusPod is available to every student at Tempe High School.
